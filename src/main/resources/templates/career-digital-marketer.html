A Digital Marketer is responsible for promoting products, services, or brands online using digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. This role is essential for driving online visibility, engagement, and conversions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan and execute digital marketing campaigns
  • Manage social media platforms and content
  • Track and analyze website traffic using tools like Google Analytics
  • Conduct SEO/SEM strategies
  • Run email marketing and PPC campaigns
  • Create marketing reports and suggest improvements

Skills Required

  • SEO & SEM Fundamentals
  • Google Ads, Google Analytics
  • Social Media Marketing (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Email Marketing Tools (Mailchimp, HubSpot)
  • Content Creation and Copywriting
  • Basic Design using Canva or Adobe tools
