A UI/UX Designer focuses on designing user-friendly interfaces and creating engaging user experiences. They ensure digital products are intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing.

  • Conduct user research and create personas

  • Test usability and collect feedback
  • Work closely with developers and stakeholders

Skills Required

  • Design principles & color theory
  • User research & testing
  • Knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus
